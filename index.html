Our Story

The LA RC Hub & Cafe Experience

Founded on a shared obsession for mechanical perfection and exceptional coffee, LA RC Hub & Cafe is the region's first venue to bridge the gap between high-end RC motorsport and artisanal café culture.

Our tracks are meticulously designed for precision racing with telemetry timing systems and VIP pit areas. Meanwhile, our baristas pull espresso shots from rare single-origin beans — ensuring that whether you're behind the transmitter or a cup of coffee, you experience nothing but the best.
