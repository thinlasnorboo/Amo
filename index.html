Our Story

The Pinnacle of RC Racing & Café Culture

Born from a passion for precision and a love of exceptional coffee, RC Track Café redefines what a leisure venue can be. We've crafted a space where motorsport enthusiasts, coffee connoisseurs, and luxury experience seekers find common ground.

Every corner of our venue has been meticulously designed — from the championship-grade track surfaces to the hand-crafted espresso stations, every detail speaks to our obsession with excellence.

  • Championship-grade asphalt track surfaces with LED lighting
  • Fleet of 1:10 scale professional RC vehicles available for hire
  • Single-origin, specialty-grade coffee menu curated by award-winning baristas
  • Private event hosting for up to 150 guests with bespoke packages
  • Live race-day broadcasts on curated screen installations

What We Offer

Curated Experiences,
Crafted for Excellence

Every service we offer is designed with one goal: to deliver an unparalleled experience that you'll want to relive again and again.

RC Track Racing

Take to our championship-grade circuit in a professionally maintained 1:10 scale RC car. Solo sessions or head-to-head racing with real-time lap timing and leaderboards.

From AED 85 / 30 min

Artisan Coffee Bar

Explore 47 signature beverages crafted by our award-winning baristas. From Ethiopian single-origin pour-overs to house-blend espresso creations — every cup is a masterpiece.

From AED 28 / cup

Private Events

Transform our venue into your exclusive domain. Corporate events, birthday celebrations, team-building experiences, and product launches — all elevated to extraordinary.

From AED 3,500 / event

Coaching & Clinics

Elevate your racing technique with one-on-one coaching from our resident racing experts. From novice orientation to advanced cornering mastery — tailored to your level.

From AED 200 / session

RC Car Customisation

Our in-house workshop offers full vehicle customisation — body kits, performance tuning, paint schemes, and livery design. Drive something that's unmistakably yours.

From AED 450 / package

Championship League
